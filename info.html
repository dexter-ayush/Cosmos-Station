How to Use Cosmos Station

A quick guide to exploring the interactive solar system and understanding what you see.

Using the Animation

  • Go to the Explore page from the top navigation.
  • Move your mouse over planets to see their names.
  • Click on any planet or the Sun to open detailed information on the right side.

What You Will Learn

  • Type of each celestial body (star, terrestrial planet, gas giant, etc.).
  • Short description of each planet.
  • Important quick facts: size, atmosphere, orbit, and special features.

Tips for Students

  • Use the site as a visual revision tool before exams.
  • Combine it with your textbook or class notes for deeper understanding.
  • Try to remember at least 2–3 facts about each planet in one sitting.
