Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System, after Jupiter. It is a gas giant with an average radius of about nine and a half times that of Earth.

It has the most extensive ring system of any planet in the Solar System. These rings are made up of ice particles, rocky debris, and dust. While other planets have rings, Saturn's are by far the most prominent and well-known.
