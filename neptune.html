Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest-known planet from the Sun in the Solar System. It is the fourth-largest planet by diameter, the third-most-massive planet, and the densest giant planet.

Neptune's atmosphere is notable for its active and visible weather patterns. These dynamic storms, such as the "Great Dark Spot," are driven by the strongest winds in the Solar System, reaching speeds of over 2,000 km/h (1,200 mph).
